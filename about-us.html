About BURHANI EXPORTS.

Burhani exports was founded in 2003 and is currently dealing in the exports of material from INDIA to Other countries. Now the company have taken a step forward in the field of solar energy.

We make clean energy available to homeowners, businesses, schools, non-profits and government organizations at a lower cost than they pay for energy generated by burning fossil fuels like coal, oil and natural gas. Our approach is to install systems to the highest engineering standards while making the switch simple for our customers. We've revolutionized the way energy is delivered by giving customers a cleaner, more affordable alternative to their monthly utility bill. We are able to install systems on residential roofs, metal buildings, and ground mounts, so we're always able to find an installation that works for your property. New advancements in solar technology have created opportunities that simply did not exist years ago. We specialize in residential and commercial solar systems – solar electric, solar water heater and solar pumps.

The work is being handled by the team of professionals and engineers who have vast experience in the field of business development, are technically sound and ensure great support and service to the end clients.

Why choose Us.

We put customers first and strive to provide a world-class customer experience.

BURHANI EXPORTS is committed towards providing high quality, affordable renewable energy and energy efficiency solutions to individuals.

We offer integrated sales, design, installation, monitoring and efficiency services from a single source, without requiring the services of multiple third-parties.

We provide excellent after sales service. We are committed to work with you to best meet your needs long after your solar project is completed. We stay on top of current developments and are constantly maximizing the energy delivered by your system to ensure we can provide you with quality, affordable service.

Our Mission.

Our goal is to help make the world a better place for future generations by creating a cleaner,safer, and more sustainable energy environment.

We are dedicated to providing renewable, non-polluting energy options for consumers while educating them on how to save money in the process. By doing so, we hope to lessen the strain we are placing on finite sources of energy and instead, focus on clean and affordable renewables.

What we Do.

In today’s climate of growing energy needs and increasing environmental concerns, turning to solar power is the solution. The sun is capable of producing the raw power required to satisfy the energy needs of every human being on Earth.

Burhani Exports makes it easy for customers to switch to cleaner energy. We take a comprehensive look at our customers' energy usage and identify opportunities for improvements.

We perform the entire process, from permitting through installation, and provide ongoing monitoring and repair services. Our project management and performance monitoring systems scale to support our clients, and provide them a real-time view of their net energy consumption.

From design and engineering services we also provide upgrade and service recommendations to improve existing systems, permitting services, and finally installation supervision services to ensure finished systems that can meet their full potential.
